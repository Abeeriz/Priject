OUR HISTORY
Our story began in 2023 when the founder, Abeer, started searching for unique models and elegant bags, when she decided to create her own brand of special models of hand-made bags with high quality.
And we made our first collection ROXA bags.


ROXA bag is handcrafted in Ylang napa leather, smooth and soft, featuring the quilted Soufflé. Finished with an adjustable strap, it can be worn over the shoulder or cross body.
ROXA collection is characterized by a great diversity of designs, inspired by a little girl who is always changing her doll's outfits. This unique bag is handcrafted in our Jordanian workshops.
